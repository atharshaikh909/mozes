The young generation is full of creativity, and passion, always ready to take risks, and excited to work hard to achieve success independently. Two such ambitious youth is Mozes Gadker and Shivkumar Tiwari, an entrepreneur, well-known digital marketing experts, and most important childhood friends. Today, they both are popularly known as the youngest social media marketer and founders of Ontogen Digital- One of the most popular Digital Marketing agencies located in Pune. Being childhood friends to business partners, they worked together and struggled a lot to achieve all this fortune. At the age of 18, They begin to achieve a great experience in business and achieved a lot that will inspire today's Genz and Millenials.

Both born in 2000 has always been a curious kid who was always in search of learning new things related to business. So how did they decide and developed their interest in digital marketing peak? Well, nowadays, we all have social media, and we use it for fun, sharing pics, communicating with friends, etc. But both of these tycoons have business minds. They enjoyed a lot using those platforms plus learned how these platforms can be used to promote various brands and businesses. They made themselves an expert to help other businesses all over the world and made many other people grow.

It's too hard to learn the dynamics of the online world at this much small age and become one of the best digital marketing experts. After becoming thorough with everything, They established their digital marketing company named 'Ontogen Digital PVT. LTD.' in 2019 and legally registered in 2022. It has been almost 3 years since their digital marketing agency started, and it seems that their business is growing and reaching at the next level.

If that achievement wasn't enough, the young entrepreneur decided to start several other subsidiaries with the parent company. With time, entrepreneur Mozes Gadker also decided to expand his skills and decided to learn about sales and marketing. After a few months of practice and consistency, He became one of the best sales experts in India. Digital Marketing Expert and Sales Expertise Mozes Gadker and Shivkumar Tiwari's journey has been inspiring and full of learning. There faced many difficulties and challenges too when things didn't work quite well, as they were a beginner, but nothing stopped them from doing their best. Their dedication and focus have really helped them accomplish in life.
